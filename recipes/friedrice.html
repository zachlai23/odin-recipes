Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables(ex. carrots, peas, green beans, ...)
  • meat(ex. chicken, shrimp, ...)
  • eggs
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Put some oil in the pan
  2. Add the egg and scramble
  3. Wait a couple minutes, add the rice and mix
  4. After mixed, add the vegetables and meat and stir together
  5. Mix soy sauce in
